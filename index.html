About Me

I am a dedicated scholar with a passion for applied research. My expertise lies in analyzing complex data sets to uncover insights and trends, which I then use to inform my research on health economics, labor economics, and health policy. Alongside my research, I am committed to teaching and sharing my knowledge with others. I strive to make a positive impact in my field and am always eager to learn and grow.
